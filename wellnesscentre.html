Wellness Centre

We are committed to providing the highest quality of care to our patients. We believe that everyone deserves access to affordable, comprehensive healthcare, and we are proud to serve the Nairobi community with our wide range of therapies and services.

Nutritional Counseling: Diet plays a crucial role in managing both diabetes and obesity. Nutritionists or dietitians work with patients to develop personalized meal plans that promote weight loss, stabilize blood sugar levels, and improve overall health. They may also offer cooking classes and grocery store tours to educate patients on making healthier food choices.

Medical Supervision and Monitoring: Trained medical professionals, such as endocrinologists, dietitians, and nurses, oversee the care of diabetic and obese patients. Regular check-ups, monitoring of blood sugar levels, and other vital signs are conducted to track progress and adjust treatment plans as necessary.

Physical Activity Programs: Exercise is essential for managing both conditions. Wellness centers often offer supervised exercise programs tailored to the needs and abilities of diabetic and obese individuals. These programs may include aerobic exercises, strength training, flexibility exercises, and low-impact activities to minimize strain on joints.

Behavioral Therapy and Counseling: Emotional well-being and mental health are vital aspects of managing chronic conditions like diabetes and obesity. Counseling services are available to help patients cope with the psychological challenges associated with their conditions, such as stress, anxiety, depression, and body image issues.
